Our Story

DMC FOOD PRODUCTS was founded by Mr. N. H. Priyaranjan with a simple vision: to bring fresh, hygienic, and tasty dairy products to every household in Sri Lanka.

Starting from our facility in Pannipitiya, we have grown into a trusted local brand known for quality and authenticity. Every product is crafted with care, using traditional methods combined with modern hygiene standards.

We believe that good dairy starts with happy cows, clean environments, and passionate people. That's why we work closely with local farmers and maintain strict quality control at every step.

Mr. N. H. Priyaranjan

Founder & Managing Director
